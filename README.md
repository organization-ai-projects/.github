# organization-ai-projects

Welcome to **organization-ai-projects** — an open-source organization building cutting-edge AI, automation, and developer tooling, primarily in **Rust**.

---

## 🚀 About Us

We are a community of engineers and researchers exploring the frontiers of artificial intelligence, neurosymbolic systems, and scalable automation platforms. Our work spans from low-level AI infrastructure to high-level developer tooling and simulation environments.

---

## 🧠 Focus Areas

- **Artificial Intelligence** — Machine learning, deep learning, and neurosymbolic AI systems
- **Automation** — Scalable developer automation, CI/CD helpers, and observability tooling
- **Simulation** — Universe and environment simulations for AI research and training
- **Developer Tooling** — CLI tools, WebSocket orchestration, and modular platform components
- **Rust Ecosystem** — Performance-first implementations leveraging Rust's safety and speed

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Primary Language**: [Rust](https://www.rust-lang.org/)
- **ML Framework**: [Burn](https://github.com/tracel-ai/burn) (with custom forks for core, nn, optim, and tensor modules)
- **Communication**: gRPC, WebSocket
- **Secondary Languages**: Python, TypeScript, HTML

## 🤝 Contributing

We welcome contributions from the community! To get started:

1. Browse our [repositories](https://github.com/orgs/organization-ai-projects/repositories) and find a project that interests you.
2. Read the project's `README.md` and contributing guidelines.
3. Open an issue or pick up an existing one.
4. Submit a pull request with your changes.

Please follow our code of conduct and respect the collaborative spirit of the organization.
